Why CodeFuture?

Personalized learning

Students meet with an instructor once a week during the course. They'll also be able to communicate directly with their instructor anytime during the week while they're going through the modules.

Accessible from anywhere

All your child needs is a laptop with a webcam and internet. Only one required hour of one-on-one instruction each week, with online learning modules to be completed on the student's own availability and time.

Creating independent coders

We educate students to the point that they're able to go out and apply software engineering to any field they end up choosing as a career, even if it's not STEM. The world is quickly being automated by software, and we want to teach students to be ahead of the curve.

What Our Students Learn

Computer Science

Our students get an early headstart on essential Computer Science topics such as algorithms, data structures, and software design.

Front-end Development

In this day and age, users expect much more than just a simple web page. We thoroughly teach HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as taking a deep dive into modern technologies such as React.js.

Back-end Development

With the rise of Node.js and its ecosystem of libraries, JavaScript has become the leader of back-end web development. We teach everything from creating a server to designing an API.

Databases

Students will also become familiar with modern databases such as MySQL and MongoDB. They will also learn best practices for integrating these into their apps.
